ABOUT US

Training built for real learning, not just resumes.

Wings Over L.A. is a full-service flight school at Van Nuys airport. Whether you're chasing a lifelong dream, building hours toward an airline career, or just want to feel the wheels leave the ground once, our instructors are happy to help you.

Excellent facilities, a modern well-maintained fleet, and clear blue-sky training days almost year round. You'll be surprised how quickly "someday" becomes "solo."
